in MVC 5 Microsoft introduced attribute routing,
which makes it easier and cleaner to create customer routes


in the routes//

routes.MapMVCAttributeRoutes();



in the controller //

[Route("movies/released/{year:regex(\\d{4})}/{month:regex(\\d{2}):range(1, 12)}")]
